# Superseded

This document described an earlier state of the Gambatte hwtests floor (16
failures) using a "twin contradiction" framing that later evidence corrected:
the sibling dumper ROMs are distinct phase-probe builds, and the true
impossibility mechanism is cross-session capture disagreement at cells the
probe deltas cannot reach.

The current, evidence-tagged account of every remaining failure (9 in the
gambatte suite, 4 in gbmicrotest) lives in
[KNOWN_FAILURES.md](KNOWN_FAILURES.md).
